Core Technology

What Makes Our Machines Work

High-Temperature Dehydration

Forced-airflow drying combined with high-temperature sterilization reduces organic waste volume by up to 96%.

Intelligent Sensing Technology

Dynamic load and moisture sensors auto-adjust power and cycle time (3–12 hrs) based on waste volume — optimizing electricity use.

Activated Carbon Deodorization

Turbine exhaust fan routes odor-laden steam through an activated carbon module. 400-hour filter life with replacement reminder.

24V DC Brushless Motor

Premium die-cast aluminum grinding module with rotating blades. Quiet operation ≤45 dB (≤35 dB flagship). 8,000-hour rated lifetime.
