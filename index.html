Shihao Intelligent Technology is a leading electric kitchen composter manufacturer and electric food-waste composter factory based in Ronggui, Shunde, Foshan, Guangdong, China — the heart of China's small-appliance manufacturing industry. Our vertically integrated facility (18,000+ sqm, 6 assembly lines, 8,000 units/day) produces the full range of household electric food-waste disposer from 2L compact to 5.5L flagship, all powered by 24V BLDC brushless DC motors, equipped with food-grade stainless steel nano-ceramic glaze inner buckets, and achieving a documented 96% food waste volume reduction rate. Pre-built certifications — PSE (Japan), ETL/UL (North America), KC (Korea), CE + ERP (Europe) — are available to all OEM/ODM partners without separate certification projects.
