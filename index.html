FAQ

B2B Buying & OEM/ODM Support

Does your factory comply with the latest EU environmental regulations?

Yes. All new Shihao platforms are engineered to meet the EU ErP eco-design standard, ensuring standby power is strictly under 0.5W. Detailed ErP and EMC test reports can be shared upon request through our inquiry form.

How does the "Intelligent Sensing" technology benefit the end-user?

Our proprietary sensors detect the humidity and weight of the waste in real-time. Instead of a fixed timer, the machine adjusts its power curve to stop exactly when dehydration is complete, saving energy and extending the filter life.

What materials are used in the grinding module?

We use Die-Cast Aluminum for our grinding modules, combined with high-torque BLDC motors. This allows the machine to process tough organic waste, including poultry bones and fruit pits, with a 96% volume reduction rate.
