Shihao Intelligent Technology

Founded in Shunde, Guangdong – the home of China's small appliance industry – Shihao Intelligent specializes in R&D, manufacturing, and global export of electric food waste disposers. We serve 20+ countries with 25+ certified models and full OEM/ODM capabilities.

Our 8,000m² facility houses advanced assembly lines, a dedicated engineering team, and in-house testing labs. From compact 2L units to industrial-grade 5.5L models, every product is built to meet regional standards (PSE, ETL, CE, KC, UL, etc.).

We believe in long-term partnerships – offering flexible MOQ, private labeling, and custom mold development. Your brand, our engineering.
