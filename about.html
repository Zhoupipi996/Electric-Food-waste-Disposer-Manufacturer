Guangdong Shihao Intelligent Technology Co., Ltd. — the specialist OEM/ODM manufacturer behind Asia-Pacific's leading electric kitchen composter brands.

Company Overview

From Appliance Maker to Category Leader

Founded in August 2020 as Zhongshan Shihao Electrical Appliance Co., Ltd., the company began producing air-circulation fans and humidifiers. By 2022, we had identified the electric kitchen composter as a high-growth premium appliance category with no dominant OEM supplier — and rebuilt our engineering team around it.

In May 2024, the company restructured as Guangdong Shihao Intelligent Technology Co., Ltd., reflecting expansion into intelligent sensing, automated assembly, and multi-market certification programs.

Today Shihao products move through B2B channels to Korea, Japan, Taiwan, North America, Europe, and Southeast Asia — serving major platform sellers, appliance brands, and retail chains.

Manufacturing Facilities

Vertical Integration — One Roof

Every core component — motor, circuit board, plastic housing, and mold — is produced on-site. This eliminates supply-chain risk and keeps quality control entirely in-house.

Mold Factory

CNC machining and EDM in-house. Average new mold cycle: 30–45 days. Precision tooling for all plastic housing components.

Motor Factory

24V DC motors wound and assembled on-site. Torque and acoustic testing performed per batch before delivery to assembly lines.

PCB Factory

Circuit board design, SMT assembly, and firmware flashing. Supports region-specific firmware for voltage and certification compliance.

Injection Molding

12 injection molding machines producing housings, buckets, lids, and filter casings. ABS and PP compounds; food-contact grades available.

Quality Lab

Soundproof room, high/low-temp chambers, salt spray tester, vibration tester, colorimeter, and constant-humidity chamber on site.

Final Assembly

6 assembly lines with UV laser engraving, automated screw fastening, and silicone component assembly. 8,000 units/day standard.
