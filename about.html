About Shihao: China's Dedicated
Electric Kitchen Composter Manufacturer Guangdong Shihao Intelligent Technology Co., Ltd. — the vertically integrated electric food-waste composter factory behind tier-one appliance brands across Korea, Japan, North America, Europe, and Southeast Asia. Established 2020 · Foshan, Guangdong, China.

Founded in August 2020 as Zhongshan Shihao Electrical Appliance Co., Ltd., Shihao entered the electric kitchen composter category in 2022 — identifying it as a high-growth segment with no dominant vertically integrated OEM supplier. We restructured in May 2024 as Guangdong Shihao Intelligent Technology Co., Ltd., reflecting our investment in intelligent sensing technology, automated assembly, and multi-market certification programs.

Today Shihao is a specialist electric food-waste composter manufacturer exclusively focused on this product vertical. We do not make humidifiers, fans, or other appliances as a core business. Every engineering investment, every certification, every mold and motor — is built around one product category: household electric food-waste composters, from 2L compact models to 5.5L flagships.

Our products reach consumers across Korea, Japan, Taiwan, North America, Europe, and Southeast Asia through 40+ OEM/ODM brand partners — serving platform sellers, premium appliance brands, retail chains, and sustainable-living distributors who require a certified electric kitchen composter factory they can trust at scale.

Why Shihao Is the Specialist Electric Kitchen Composter Manufacturer

Most OEM appliance factories manufacture dozens of unrelated product categories. Shihao manufactures electric food-waste composters — and only electric food-waste composters — as its core product. This vertical specialisation means our engineering team's accumulated knowledge, our testing laboratory's calibration, our motor factory's BLDC optimisation, and our mold shop's tooling experience are all concentrated in one product domain.

The result: 96% food waste reduction rate, ≤35 dB flagship noise (the quietest in the category), stainless steel nano-ceramic inner buckets across all SKUs, and pre-built certifications covering five major markets — delivered from a 200-unit MOQ with 30-day sample lead time.

Every core component of a Shihao electric food-waste composter — the 24V BLDC motor, the stainless steel nano-ceramic bucket, the circuit board, the plastic housing — is designed and manufactured in-house at our 18,000+ sqm Foshan facility. This eliminates the supply chain risks that affect assembler-type OEM factories and gives tier-one brand partners the traceability and quality control they require.
