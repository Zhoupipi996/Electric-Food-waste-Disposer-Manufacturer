Shihao Intelligent Technology is in the second group. This article details exactly what that means in practice for a tier-one B2B brand partner.

3. Sample SLA: 30 Days to a Branded Sample on Your Desk

Lead time from inquiry to branded sample is the metric that determines whether a brand can hit their retail season window. Shihao's sample commitments for tier-one partners:

  1. Day 1–3: Inquiry Response & Model Recommendation

    Submit your brief — target market, preferred capacity, intended volume. Our B2B team responds within 1 business day with specific model recommendations, indicative pricing, and a timeline. NDA can be signed immediately on request.

  2. Day 3–7: Specification Lock & Sample Order Confirmation

    Confirm: model code, colour, logo placement, packaging specification, target market voltage and certification, and language requirements for documentation. Sample invoice issued. Payment received.

  3. Day 7–30: Sample Production & Pre-Shipment Review

    Branded sample produced in our Foshan facility. Pre-shipment photos and video shared for visual approval before courier dispatch. Express courier (DHL/FedEx) tracking provided on dispatch.

  4. Day 30–45: Partner Evaluation & Feedback

    Partner conducts functional evaluation. Shihao provides a structured Sample Evaluation Report (SER) template covering: reduction rate, noise, cleaning ease, coating integrity, branding compliance, and documentation review.

  5. Day 45–55: Sample Approval & Mass Production Launch

    Signed Sample Approval Confirmation (SAC) locks the production specification. PO issued. Mass production begins within 5 business days of PO receipt.

For ODM projects requiring new mold development, the timeline extends to 45–60 days to prototype tooling, followed by an additional 20–25 days to sample. Full ODM projects are managed with a dedicated product development timeline shared with the partner at project kick-off.

6. Vertical Integration: Why It Makes Shihao a Structurally Different Partner

The supply chain risk that tier-one brands most frequently encounter with OEM sourcing is second-tier quality failure — the OEM assembler's sub-supplier changed a component, or a motor batch was substituted without notification. Shihao's vertical integration eliminates this class of risk:

  • Mold factory (in-house): All tooling designed and cut in Foshan. No third-party mold shop. Average new mold: 30–45 days.
  • Motor factory (in-house): 24V BLDC motors wound, assembled, and torque/acoustics-tested on-site. No third-party motor supplier substitution risk.
  • PCB factory (in-house): Circuit board design, SMT assembly, firmware flashing, and regional firmware variants all produced internally. Specification changes are controlled and notified.
  • Injection molding (in-house): 12 machines producing housing bodies, buckets, lids, filter casings in ABS and PP. Food-contact grades available.
  • Quality laboratory (in-house): Soundproof testing room, high/low-temperature chamber, salt spray tester, vibration tester, multi-channel temperature tester, colorimeter, and constant-temperature/humidity chamber on site.

When a component specification changes — for any reason — Shihao's warranty letter requires us to notify the partner immediately, submit updated drawings, and obtain approval before recommencing production. Partners are never surprised by a substitution after delivery.
