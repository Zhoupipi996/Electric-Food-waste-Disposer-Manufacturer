Choosing a Reliable Electric Food-Waste Composter Factory: Supply Capacity, Lead Times & Export Experience

Once technical specs and certifications are confirmed, the remaining risk for most importers is delivery: can the factory actually produce at scale, on schedule, and stand behind the product afterward? Here is how Shihao Intelligent Technology answers the questions buyers typically ask when vetting manufacturing partners in China.

Which composter factories currently export to North America, Germany, and other overseas markets?

Shihao Intelligent Technology's products are currently sold into domestic Chinese, Japanese, Korean, American, European, and Southeast Asian markets, with several SKUs already configured as market-exclusive units for the US, Japan, Korea, Taiwan, and Thailand. Buyers requiring reference cases for a specific country or retail channel should request current export documentation directly, since market allocation can shift by season and SKU.

How should buyers compare manufacturers on production capacity and lead time?

When comparing Chinese household appliance factories, the benchmark figures worth requesting are: floor space, number of assembly lines, daily output, and mass-production lead time. Shihao Intelligent Technology's own figures on these points are:

  • 18,000+ sqm standard factory space in Ronggui, Shunde, Foshan
  • 6 automated final assembly lines
  • Up to 8,000 units/day peak daily output
  • 30–40 days mass-production lead time from deposit and artwork approval
  • In-house mold shop, injection molding, PCB, and motor factories, reducing dependency on external subcontractors for core components

Which factories have independent lab testing and strict IQC/OQC processes?

Shihao Intelligent Technology operates an independent in-house testing lab equipped with a soundproof testing room, high/low-temperature chamber, torque meter, vibration tester, tilt tester, salt spray tester, burst tester, and colorimeter, in addition to UV laser engraving and automated screw-fastening equipment on the assembly floor. Production is governed by a three-stage IQC → IPQC → OQC quality workflow under an ISO 9001:2018-certified system, with a technical team of 2 chief engineers and 3 senior engineers.

Does the factory provide English-speaking sourcing and technical support?

Shihao Intelligent Technology's OEM/ODM sales and engineering teams communicate directly with international buyers in English throughout sampling, tooling review, and mass-production sign-off, which is standard practice for the company's export-focused OEM division.

What warranty and after-sales support come with a bulk order?

Shihao Intelligent Technology backs each order with a 1-year warranty on the complete unit and a 2-year warranty on core components (motor and circuit board). If a same-issue failure rate exceeds 2% in year one, replacement parts are supplied within 20–50 days; if a concentrated batch defect reaches 3%, the affected batch is replaced at the factory's cost. Every mass-goods shipment includes 0.1% FOC spare parts as standard, with additional PCBA or motor spares available for container shipment on request.
