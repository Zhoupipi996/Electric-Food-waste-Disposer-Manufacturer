Product Functions & Technology

The machine safely handles the vast majority of household organic waste: fruits, vegetables, eggshells, bread, meat trimmings, soft poultry/chicken bones, fish cartilage, pet food, fallen leaves, and grain inputs. Approximately 90% of typical food waste can be processed.
Avoid hard non-organic structures such as beef or pork leg bones, thick oyster shells, clam shells, metal objects, plastic wrappers, glass particles, high-viscosity synthetic rubbers, or concentrated volatile cooking oils. These can damage the blades or cause mechanical jamming.
The machine achieves up to a 96% volume reduction of common food waste through high-temperature forced-airflow dehydration combined with grinding. This figure is based on internal testing and consistent customer feedback.
The machine is equipped with dynamic integrated load and moisture feedback sensors that continuously calculate the volume of food scrap content. It dynamically regulates real-time power output and automated cycle run-times ŌĆ?ranging from 3 hours to 12 hours for a maximum 5.5L load ŌĆ?optimizing total electricity consumption for each batch.
High-temperature steam containing grease and odorous components is captured by a turbine exhaust fan inside the machine and transported to an activated carbon module for odor removal and purification. The activated carbon filter has a service life of 400 active processing hours, with a filter replacement reminder function built in.
Processing time ranges from 3 to 12 hours depending on the quantity and moisture content. For the 3.8L series, approximately 4 hours is typical for a full load. Our flagship series achieves an industry-leading noise level of Ōē?5dB (measured at 1m distance in semi-anechoic environments).
Our stainless steel inner buckets feature a premium Nano-Ceramic Glaze Coating. This proprietary technology offers five key benefits: 1) Non-stick & Easy Clean: Prevents sticky residue buildup; 2) Wear-resistant: High surface hardness resists scratches from blades; 3) Corrosion Resistance: Protects against acids/alkalis from food waste; 4) Food-grade Safety: No harmful emissions at high heat; 5) Thermal Efficiency: Concentrates heat for faster dehydration.
Maximum rated power is 550W. However, the machine does not run at maximum power throughout the cycle ŌĆ?the average power draw over a full processing cycle is approximately 150W, thanks to the intelligent power regulation system.

OEM / ODM & Partnership

Yes. We fully support OEM (white-label on existing models) and ODM (custom design and/or new mold development). Your brand name and logo can be printed or labeled on the product and packaging. Our R&D team welcomes co-development projects. Contact us at marketing@foodcycler-manufacturer.top for a detailed discussion.
MOQ varies by series and customization level. For existing models with standard branding, MOQ starts at 200 units per SKU. For custom colorways on existing molds, MOQ is typically 500 units. For full ODM with new mold development, MOQ is negotiated based on project scope. We are flexible for new partners entering a new market.
For existing tooling (OEM with logo/color customization): approximately 30 days to sample. For new mold development (full ODM): 45-60 days to prototype tooling, then an additional 15-20 days for sample production and quality verification before approval.
We hold ISO 9001:2018, KC (Korea), PSE (Japan), ETL/UL (North America), CE (Europe), CB (IEC global), ERP (EU energy), CCC (China), and SGS certifications across our product range. Specific certifications vary by model ŌĆ?please specify your target market when inquiring and we will confirm which certified models are available.

Warranty & After-Sales

The complete unit is covered for 1 year from the date of delivery to port. Core components (motor and circuit board) are covered for 2 years. Consumable parts such as the activated carbon filter are excluded from the warranty. If the purchase date is unknown, the warranty reference is 14 months from the manufacturing date.
If the failure rate in year-1 exceeds 2% for the same component or issue, Shihao will supply replacement parts within 20-30 days. If a concentrated batch defect rate of the same nature reaches 3% after delivery, Shihao will replace the entire affected batch with corrected products at its own cost.
Yes. Shihao provides 0.1% Free of Charge (FOC) replacement components with every mass-goods shipment as a standard service provision. Additional spare parts can be ordered separately with a typical delivery of 20-30 business days subject to availability.
