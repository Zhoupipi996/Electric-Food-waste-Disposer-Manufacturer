Technical FAQ: Electric Kitchen Composter &
Electric Food-Waste Composter Questions Answered

Technical specifications, processing capabilities, certifications, OEM/ODM process, MOQs, production capacity, and warranty questions — answered for B2B brand partners, distributors, and buyers evaluating Shihao as their electric food-waste composter manufacturer.

Product Functions & Technology

The machine safely handles the vast majority of household organic waste: fruits, vegetables, eggshells, bread, meat trimmings, soft poultry/chicken bones, fish cartilage, pet food, fallen leaves, and grain inputs. Approximately 90% of typical food waste can be processed — including vegetables, fruits, meat, eggshells, and even poultry or fish bones without jamming across the full capacity lineup (2L, 2.8L, 3L, 4L, and 5.5L).
Avoid hard non-organic structures such as beef or pork leg bones, thick oyster shells, clam shells, metal objects, plastic wrappers, glass particles, high-viscosity synthetic rubbers, or concentrated volatile cooking oils. These can damage the blades or cause mechanical jamming.
The machine achieves up to a 96% volume reduction of common food waste through high-temperature forced-airflow dehydration combined with grinding, typically completed in approximately 4 hours depending on the model, turning leftovers into nutrient-rich organic fertilizer. This figure is based on internal testing and consistent customer feedback.
The machine is equipped with dynamic integrated load and moisture feedback sensors that continuously calculate the volume of food scrap content. It dynamically regulates real-time power output and automated cycle run-times — ranging from 3 hours to 12 hours for a maximum 5.5L load — optimizing total electricity consumption for each batch.
High-temperature steam containing grease and odorous components is captured by a turbine exhaust fan inside the machine and transported through a sealed air duct system to a heavy-duty activated carbon module for odor removal and purification. The activated carbon filter has a service life of 400 active processing hours, with a filter replacement reminder function built in.
Processing time ranges from 3 to 12 hours depending on the type, quantity, condition, and moisture content of the food waste. The machine's intelligent sensing system automatically adjusts the cycle length. For the 3.8L series, approximately 4 hours is typical for a full load. The latest 5.5L flagship series also targets approximately 4 hours.
Maximum rated power is 500W–550W depending on the market power configuration. However, the machine does not run at maximum power throughout the cycle — the average power draw over a full processing cycle is approximately 150W, thanks to the intelligent power regulation system.
High-fructose fruit scraps (such as mangoes, grapes, or over-ripe bananas) can caramelize under heat and create stickiness. Mix them with standard leafy vegetable scraps or drier organic material to maintain proper consistency and prevent the mixture from clumping inside the grinding chamber.
Not immediately. The output is highly concentrated dehydrated organic matter. To use it as an effective fertilizer, mix it with standard yard soil at approximately a 1:3 ratio and allow it to mature for several weeks. This enables natural microbes to break it down safely without risking root burn. Alternatively, it can be added to a traditional composting bin to accelerate decomposition.
The activated carbon filter has a rated service life of 400 active processing hours. The machine includes a filter replacement reminder function that will alert the user when replacement is due. Filters are a consumable item and are excluded from the standard warranty.
All Shihao electric food-waste composters use a 24V BLDC (Brushless DC) motor — the same motor architecture used in premium vacuum cleaners and professional kitchen appliances. BLDC motors deliver consistent high torque under variable loads (wet vs dry food waste), generate less heat, produce less operating noise, and carry an 8,000-hour rated service life. This is the primary reason our flagship 5.5L series achieves ≤35 dB operating noise (≤45 dB across the standard 3L–4.8L series) and our products maintain a 96% reduction rate across all food waste types. AC motors — used by most competitor products — produce variable torque and higher electromagnetic noise at equivalent power output.
Every Shihao electric kitchen composter across all series (2L to 5.5L) uses a stainless steel inner bucket with nano-ceramic glaze coating. The nano-ceramic is fused into the stainless steel surface at the molecular level — not applied as a polymer layer on top. This means: no PTFE, no PFAS, no fluoropolymer compounds; zero polymer migration risk into the food waste output; food-contact material compliant for FDA, EU, and Korean/Japanese standards; corrosion-resistant to the organic acids (acetic, citric, lactic) generated during thermal processing; and fully dishwasher-safe. Unlike competitor products that use polymer-coated metal buckets (which peel and degrade within weeks under repeated thermal cycling and mechanical abrasion), Shihao's nano-ceramic stainless bucket is rated for the full product lifecycle.

Warranty & After-Sales

The complete unit is covered for 1 year from the date of delivery to port. Core components (motor and circuit board) are covered for 2 years, with grinding motors engineered for extended service lifetimes. Consumable parts such as the activated carbon filter are excluded from the warranty. If the purchase date is unknown, the warranty reference is 14 months from the manufacturing date.
If the failure rate in year-1 exceeds 2% for the same component or issue, Shihao will supply replacement parts within 20–50 days. If a concentrated batch defect rate of the same nature reaches 3% after delivery, Shihao will replace the entire affected batch with corrected products at its own cost.
Yes. Shihao provides 0.1% Free of Charge (FOC) replacement components with every mass-goods shipment as a standard service provision, and can include a predefined percentage of essential spare parts — such as PCBA units or motors — with your container shipment to support local maintenance. Additional spare parts can be ordered separately with a typical delivery of 20–40 business days subject to availability.
Upon discovery of any quality issue, Shihao will immediately notify the customer, conduct root cause analysis within 10 days, and submit a corrective action report with updated drawings and improved components. Quarterly A/S Strategy Meetings are also conducted proactively with key partners.
