Who We Are

Vertical Integration for Global Excellence

Behind every high-performance composter is a supply chain of precision. Based in Shunde, the world's appliance capital, Shihao Intelligent Technology (founded 2020) provides the engineering backbone for the world’s leading kitchen brands.

The Shihao Advantage

100% In-House Control

We don’t just assemble; we innovate. Our 18,000 sqm Shunde facility integrates the entire manufacturing lifecycle under one roof to ensure unmatched quality stability:

  • • Mold & Injection: Custom ODM housings with high-precision tooling.
  • • PCB & Firmware: Proprietary algorithms for "Intelligent Sensing" and power curve optimization.
  • • Motor Factory: Self-developed 24V DC Brushless Motors (rated for 8,000+ hours).

This vertical integration allows us to deliver 8,000 units daily with a guaranteed year-1 failure rate of sub-2%.
